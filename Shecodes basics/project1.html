I love Sci-fi movies

Especially Marvel and DC comics

Science-fiction (Sci-fi) is a film genre that uses speculative, fictional science-based depictions of phenomena that are not fully accepted by mainstream science, such as extraterrestrial lifeforms, spacecraft, robots, cyborgs, interstellar travel or other technologies.
Sci-fi movies came into existence since the 1900s, where movies were shown in black and white. It evolved over the years till we have the suspense filled and action packed movies present today. I loves sci-fi movies because it gives me an escape from reality and also gets me thinking on what happens to an hero or villain in the movie.

Marvel and DC comics have stood out from their contemporaries based on their story(historically inspired e.g Greek mythology), cinematography, movie effects and quality. My favourites of all time include Spiderman, Superman, Wonder-woman etc.
